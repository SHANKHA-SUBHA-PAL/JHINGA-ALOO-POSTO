## ***The method of making jhinga aloo posto***
---------------------------------------------
*The ingridients to make jhinga aloo posto are given below :-*


* JHINGA(RIDGE GOURD) ->500 gm 
* POTATO ->250 gm
* ONIONS ->2 
* GREEN CHILLI ->4
* POPPY SEEDS ->10 gm
* TURMERIC POWDER ->1 teaspoon 
* SALT ->As per taste 
* SUGAR ->As per taste 
* BLACK CUMIN ->1 pinch
* DRIED RED CHILLI ->2
* OIL ->2 tablespoon
